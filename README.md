# Switchpad
Open source, 3d printable macro pad + light switch combo

# Assembly
Print:
 - 1 plate
 - 1 body

Buy:
 - 1 Raspberry Pi Pico 2
 - 8 Cherry MX Switches
 - 1 Light Switch

### step 1:
Place all of the 8 switches on the plate, solder one common ground per row
Solder diodes to the rest of the pins and solder some wire to the other end of the diodes

### step 2:
Screw in the Raspberry Pi Pico 2 and solder the ground wires to ground pins and the rest of the wires to input pins
Solder two extra wires to ground and two extra wires to input pins for the light switch

### step 3:
plug in the raspberry pi pico 2 into your pc while holding down bootsel

### step 4:
use [pog](https://github.com/JanLunge/pog) to program the keyboard and add macros to your liking

### step 5:
Get your light switch and screw in the corresponding wires to the corresponding inputs/outputs

### step 6:
screw in your light switch

### great success, enjoy your switchpad
